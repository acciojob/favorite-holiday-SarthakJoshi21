Maldives - Paradise on Earth

The Maldives is a tropical nation in the Indian Ocean, known for its stunning white sandy beaches, crystal clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs.

  1. Snorkeling in the Coral Reefs - Explore the underwater world with breathtaking marine life.
  2. Visiting Local Islands - Experience the culture and hospitality of Maldivian communities.
  3. Luxury Resorts - Indulge in world-class amenities and overwater villas.
  4. Dolphin Watching - Witness playful dolphins in their natural habitat.
  5. Sunset Cruises - Enjoy romantic evenings on a traditional Maldivian boat.
  • Mas Huni - A traditional Maldivian breakfast dish made with tuna, coconut, onion, and chili.
  • Fihunu Mas - Grilled fish marinated with local spices and served with rice.
  • Bis Keemiya - Deep-fried pastry filled with spicy tuna, onion, and coconut.
  • Garudhiya - A fish soup served with rice, lime, chili, and onions.
  • Banana Flower Salad - Salad made from banana flower mixed with grated coconut, chili, and lime juice.
